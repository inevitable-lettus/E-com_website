This is a website for my university challenge (it is a week-long project development). It needs to be user-friendly and have a good UI and UX. Also it should be responsive and should work on both desktop and mobile.
We do not need to take it live for the moment but we do need placeholder sample items on the website so that we can present it to people during the presentation.
Our idea is to local host it and expose the port of our device so that people can access it from there. 

Its name is Use(Less) - it is a website for renting out household non-perishable and non-consumable items. 



E-commerce website for household non perishable and on consummable items. 

List of features - 
Login - Oauth (google login)
wallet for monetary transactions
chatting feature between rentee and renters


Users who want to put products for others to rent - pictures of the product,
                                                    a description, 
                                                    condition of the product, 
                                                    renters agreement, 
                                                    payment recieving details, 
                                                    any special conditions
                                                    dashboard of products kept on rent by others
                                                    
Customers who want to rent products from others - renters agreement, 
                                                    submission of ID and address proof,
                                                    dashboard of rented products with return dates and penalties
